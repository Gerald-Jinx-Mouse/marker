# Slide 11

## Pricing Strategy — Data-Backed Recommendations

## Map Directions: RAISE to $5.75 (from $5.25) — captive users, inelastic demand. Only -2.9% churn = +$2,831/mo
## Toones: PROMO $0.50 off for 3 months — +14.8% users (~1,046 new). High demand elasticity = long-term revenue lift
## Bundle: "PhantomSea Essentials" @ $6.99/mo — Map + Toones (saves vs. $7.75 separate). Drives dual adoption + reduces churn
## Cat Scratchers: KEEP at $0.99 — extremely price-sensitive (+$0.50 loses 36.1% of users). Focus on volume growth
## Speedper: LAUNCH at $0.99/mo post-beta — +127% beta growth, $0 revenue today. Low price = immediate adoption

# Slide 18

## Risks & Mitigations

## Risk 1: Revenue Concentration
- Map Directions + Toones = 93% of total revenue
- Any disruption to either app = catastrophic revenue impact
- Mitigation: Diversify via 4 underperforming apps + bundle + marketplace
## Risk 2: Tzynergism Partner Dependency
- Tzynergism controls Toones + Unicorn Xplode + Speedper (3 of 6 apps)
- ~39% of total revenue flows through a single third-party partner
- Mitigation: Negotiate exclusivity; evaluate Tzynergism acquisition

## Risk 3: Declining App Portfolio
- NewNews: -50.7% YTD — every month declining
- Unicorn Xplode: -32.1% with possible Dec service outage
- Cat Scratchers: extreme month-to-month volatility
- Mitigation: Rebuild or sunset NewNews; investigate Xplode outage; stabilize Cat Scratchers
## Risk 4: Persistently Low Penetration (8.4%)
- Fleet grew +65.6% but app adoption hasn't kept pace
- New owners are not discovering or subscribing to apps
- Mitigation: Onboarding pop-ups, referral programs, app discovery features
